FAQs

Common questions answered

What is the consultation fee?▼

The standard consultation fee is ₹600 for in-clinic visits. Online consultations are also available. ECHS, Ayushman Bharat, CGHS, Golden Card, ONGC, and THDC card holders are covered under their respective schemes.

Is the consultation confidential?▼

Absolutely. 100% confidentiality is maintained for all patients. Dr. Goyal and his team understand the sensitive nature of urological conditions and ensure complete privacy at every step.

Can I consult online from outside Dehradun?▼

Yes. Dr. Goyal offers online video consultations for patients across India. Simply select "Online" when booking and our team will WhatsApp you the video link at the confirmed time.

What should I bring to my first appointment?▼

Please bring any previous medical reports, prescriptions, investigation results (blood tests, ultrasound, etc.) and your insurance / government health card if applicable.

How soon can I get an appointment?▼

In most cases, appointments are available within 24–48 hours. For urgent cases, same-day appointments may be available — please WhatsApp us directly at +91 9927012218.
